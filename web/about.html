Why the logo?

The Be DC logo is the way for DC residents to show that they are proud to live and work in DC and for visitors to show their appreciation for our beautiful city.

The message  



By saying Be DC, you demonstrate a clear choice for the city of Washington DC. Visitors, residents and businesses can express that they know they are DC.
The star behind the B stands for anything. In DC you can be anything, be in awe of all the beautiful buildings, be inspired by all the historic places and memorials, be heard by rallying, etc.

The design  



The letters of the logo represent that Washington DC is the seat of the US government. The B is the same letter-type as used in the constitution. DC is the same as the letter-type the government uses to write laws in.
The colors of the logo represent that Washington DC is the capitol of the United States of America by using the same colors as the American flag.

To come  



Big 3d models of the logo throughout the city, T-shirt sales, sticker-campaign, you-tube video of DC-residents expressing their Be DC feeling, and much more.
